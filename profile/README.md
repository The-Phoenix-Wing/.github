## 🌟 About Us

The Phoenix Wing is a dedicated online gaming and community network built for players who value cooperation, strategy, and a mature environment. We host a variety of game servers and coordinate large-scale group activities across multiple titles. 

Whether we're establishing a stronghold in a survival game, managing a fleet in the depths of space, or hanging out in voice channels, we prioritize a strong, tight-knit community experience.

## 🚀 Phoenix Wing Industries

**Phoenix Wing Industries (PWI)** is our specialized division focused on deep-space exploration, trade, and fleet operations. PWI serves as our primary organizational structure for our sci-fi simulation operations, heavily focused on:

* **Star Citizen:** Fleet coordination, resource running, and joint organizational ops.
* **Elite Dangerous:** Squadron operations, background simulation (BGS) manipulation, and deep space expeditions.

## 📜 Community Guidelines

To ensure a high-quality environment for all our members, we enforce a few core guidelines:

1. **18+ Requirement:** The Phoenix Wing is strictly an **18+ community**. All members must be of legal adult age.
2. **Respect & Maturity:** We expect all members to treat each other with respect, both in-game and on our community platforms.
3. **Active Participation:** While real life always comes first, we encourage active participation in our forums, Discord, and community events.

## 💻 Infrastructure & Servers

We take pride in our robust, self-hosted community infrastructure. Our network relies on high-performance, containerized deployments to keep everything running smoothly:

* **Game Servers:** Custom-configured, dedicated servers with optimized performance for titles like Minecraft, Rust, and Project Zomboid.
* **Community Portals:** Dedicated web hosting and forums to keep our operations organized and our members informed.
